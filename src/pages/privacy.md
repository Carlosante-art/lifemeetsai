# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: April 2026*

LifeMeetsAI is operated by Carlosante, based in Sweden. This policy explains what data I collect, why, and what I do with it.

## The short version

I collect very little. I don't sell your data. I don't run invasive tracking. If you never contact me or subscribe to anything, I barely know you exist.

## What I collect

### When you visit the site

- **Analytics data** (via Google Analytics or Plausible): anonymized information about which pages you view, how long you stay, your approximate country, and the type of device you're using. This is aggregated — I can't identify you personally from this data.
- **Server logs**: standard web server logs (IP address, browser type, timestamps) kept briefly for security and debugging.

### When you email me

- Your email address and anything you write to me. I keep this to respond and, where relevant, follow up. I don't share it.

### When you subscribe to my newsletter (if/when launched)

- Your email address. Used only to send newsletters you signed up for. Unsubscribe anytime.

### When you click affiliate links

- Retailers (like Amazon) set their own cookies to track purchases through my links. I only see aggregated commission data — not who bought what. Those cookies are governed by the retailer's own privacy policy.

## What I don't do

- I don't sell your data.
- I don't share your data with third parties except service providers strictly necessary to run the site (hosting, analytics, email).
- I don't run cross-site tracking or build advertising profiles.
- I don't use your data for AI training.

## Cookies

This site uses minimal cookies:

- **Essential cookies** for the site to function (session, preferences)
- **Analytics cookies** (anonymized)
- **Affiliate tracking cookies** set by retailers when you click their links

You can disable cookies in your browser. The site will still work.

## Your rights (GDPR)

If you're in the EU or UK, you have the right to:

- Know what data I hold about you
- Ask for a copy
- Ask me to correct or delete it
- Object to processing
- File a complaint with your data protection authority

To exercise any of these, email me: [contact@lifemeetsai.com](mailto:contact@lifemeetsai.com).

## Third-party services

The site uses:

- **Cloudflare** (hosting, DNS, CDN) — [privacy policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/)
- **Google Analytics** or **Plausible** (analytics)
- **Amazon, Impact, Awin, and other affiliate networks** (when you click affiliate links)

Each has its own privacy policy. When you interact with their services through my site, their policies apply.

## Changes

If I change this policy in a meaningful way, I'll update the "last updated" date and, where appropriate, notify subscribers.

## Contact

Questions? Email me: [contact@lifemeetsai.com](mailto:contact@lifemeetsai.com).

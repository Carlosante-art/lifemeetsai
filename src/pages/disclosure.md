# Affiliate Disclosure

*Last updated: April 2026*

## The short version

Some links on LifeMeetsAI are affiliate links. If you click one and buy something, I may earn a small commission — at no extra cost to you.

I only link to products I'd genuinely recommend to friends or family.

## The longer version

LifeMeetsAI is free to read. There are no paywalls, no ads, no sponsored posts disguised as editorial content.

To keep it free, I participate in affiliate programs, including:

- Amazon Associates
- Impact, Awin, and other affiliate networks
- Direct partnerships with brands whose products I recommend

When I link to a product — a paint brand, a piece of furniture, a tool, a service — that link often carries an affiliate tag. If you click it and buy the product, the retailer pays me a small percentage of the sale. The price you pay stays exactly the same.

## What you should know

- **I don't get paid for clicks alone.** I only earn when someone actually buys something through a link.
- **I don't recommend products I haven't researched or used.** If I haven't formed a real opinion on something, I won't recommend it.
- **Affiliate relationships never influence what I recommend.** I've turned down recommendations that would have paid better because I didn't actually like the product.
- **I identify affiliate links clearly** in my posts when it's not obvious from context.

## FTC compliance

As required by the U.S. Federal Trade Commission (FTC) and equivalent regulations in the EU and UK, I disclose any material connection I have with brands I mention. If a post includes affiliate links or a sponsored section, you'll know.

## Amazon Associates disclosure

LifeMeetsAI is a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon.com and affiliated sites.
